Volunteer Program: Help Girls Learn
Available Locations: Sudan, Uganda
Duration: Long term
Schedule: We organize trips once two months. Each trip lasts for from two weeks several months.

Help Girls Learn is a program that supports education for girls in various regions. We invite volunteers to come and teach the girls. Because we do need volunteers to teach the girls, we would like to invite more volunteers who can stay longer. However if you are interested in shorter terms teaching you can join us for the two week trips.

Volunteer Program: Feed My Starving Children
Available locations: US major cities and South Sudan
Duration: Short term
Schedule: In the US the schedule varies according to locations. Contact us if you are interested. For volunteering in South Sudan, we organize trips twice each year

Program Details: Feed My Starving Children is a program that helps malnutritioned children. We help package food in the US on a regular basis and sends volunteers to South Sudan to get updated information on what the children need.
Volunteer Program: We Need Your Help
Available Locations: Chad
Duration: Long term
Schdule: Irregular

Program Details: We Need Your Help invites volunteers who are willing to commit for more than a year. Volunteers get to choose the location they want to volunteer at. You tell us where you want to go, and we will provide you with the list of programs and the necessary trainings.
