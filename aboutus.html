About Us

We are group of Northwestern students passionate about Human Computer Interactions and volunteering.
We designed this website as part of our course project for EECS class.
This website aims to facilitate the process of donating to people in need and letting people know about volunteering opportunities.
You can sign up for volunteering, donating and contact us if you have any questions.
We tried to design the website so that it is convenient, intuitive and user-friendly. Let us know if you have any suggestions ;)
