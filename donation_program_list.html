Donation Program: Donate with our Volunteers
Location: All volunteering locations
How do I donate: Contact us with any specific requirements you may have or just click "Donate Now" and we will send you more information!

Every month we send volunteers to support girl education and pack food for malnutritioned children. In addition to their help, we also take direct donation for those unable to travel far but are willing to help. Volunteer will buy children supplies or help girls pay tuition with your donation. The best part: you know where your money is spent!

Donation Program: Keep me Posted!
Location: Everywhere!
How do I donate: Contact us with any specific requirements you may have or just click "Donate Now" and we will send you more information!

Keep me posted is a program that provides information about donation activities in the neighborhood. It is not limited to monetary donation-- it might be clothes, furnitures, anything. We post information about recent donations in the neighborhood so you can check them out very conveniently once the items you wish to donate matches an event.
